Who am I?

I am a creative person who has always been interested in the design of things, from the wrapping of a candy to the advertising campaign of a great brand. I can work on a wide range of projects, from branding and logo design to editorial design, packaging, illustration and web design. I am a perfectionist in what I do, I have a critical eye and an obsessive attention to detail. My goal is not only to meet expectations, but to exceed them in every project in which I am involved.
